Welcome to CSM

Sharing the hope of Jesus

When you walk around the village you will see something different on the faces of the Christians. They have a joy they didn't have before. -- Mathew
Welcome to the Christhia Seva Mission website. We are located in the district of Chikbalapur (Wikipedia) in the state of Karnataka, India. The mission was established in 1980 by Mathew and Mary Mathai. Since then it has grown to over 300 villages reaching more than 4,000 people.

Stories of Christians

Our mission exists to share the gospel with people who want to hear about it. You can read stories from Christians to learn more about how God is working through our mission.
